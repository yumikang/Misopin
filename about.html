Ourmission
Our fusion of modern innovations in the field of cosmetology and centuries of massage culture from Thailand and Bali makes the treatments you will try at 7 Sky Spa unique and unforgettable. We sincerely believe, that when living the big-city life, it is vital to bring your body and mind to a state of balance and harmony, charge yourself with energy, and relax during the busy weeks. At 7 Sky Spa, you can expect a calming and relaxing ambiance, premium service with complimentary drinks and light bites, and top level SPA treatments.
Private Hammam
Experience the most luxurious treatments in the pearl of our SPA, the Turkish Hammam.
Massages & Body Wrappings
Our wide range of Traditional Thai and Balinese massages feature choices for any state of the body and mind. From deeply relaxing Balinese stone massage to the Phytomer Slimming treatments, to chocolate wrappings and 4 Hands Royal Massage.
