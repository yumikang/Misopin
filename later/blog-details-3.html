Exotic Cocktail – New Seasonal Treatment

12/12/2022

The cold season is coming, and with it, come new exotic treatments. You can now try the brand new treatment Exotic Cocktail! The treatment includes:

Choice from 7 exotic scrubs
15-minute leg scrub
45-minute Balinese massage

You can pick between the scrubs: Mango, Lychee, Papaya, Grape Fruit, Green Tea, Yuzu, Lotos, and Banana.

The treatment will leave your legs smooth and shiny. Your skin will feel soft and moisturised, and the exotic scent of the scrub will improve your mood even if you were already having a great day. The relaxing Balinese massage after the scrub will allow you to rest, recover your muscles, and maybe even get some sleep in the middle of a hard day. The treatment does not require you to use the shower after completion, however, if you prefer to use the shower, feel free to use the shower in your room. At 7 Sky Spa every room has a shower, and a set of essentials such as a hairbrush, make up removal kits, male and female antiperspirant, hair drier, and other little things next to the sink and the mirror.
