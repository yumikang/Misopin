About

Four hands massage is a unique technique performed in sync by two therapists. This treatment gives you the ultimate spa experience, and leaves you with a great physical and emotional booster.

What's included in the treatment:
  • Unique techniques performed in sync specially made for four hand treatments
  • A choice from a wide range of 7 differently scented exotic oils
  • Complimentary refreshments and light bites after your treatment

The Royal Massage has more than double results from traditional massage. You will instantly feel relaxed, refreshed, and rejuvenated. This is a powerful treatment for anyone who wants an immediate indulgence into relaxationand make the most out of their time.
