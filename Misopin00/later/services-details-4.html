About

Reflexology is a foot massage that uses gentle pressure to stimulate your body’s reflex points on the feet. This technique can help stimulate nerve endings in order to reduce stress and tension. Perfect as an introductory treatment, often accompanied by a full body massage such as our Balinese/Thai Classic.

What is included in the service:
  • Light foot massage
  • Soothing reflexology session stimulating nerve ending, to assist in body’s function
  • Complimentary refreshments and light bites after your treatment

This treatment is highly recommended for Athletes or anyone working on their legs and feet a lot. If you need a short and efficient treatment to target the feet area and muscles for relaxation – this treatment is for you!

FAQ
What is different about 7 sky spa

7 Sky Spa is a Luxury City Spa offering a wide range of exotic and authentic Balinese treatments. Apart from the traditional massages, and aromatic body wrappings, you can treat yourself to the luxurious hammam treatments, pioneer facial treatment that includes an active clay mask for your back, and a choice of toning, firming, and slimming treatments with proven clinical results from Phytomer.

Most of our rooms are double, so you can have the same or different treatments together with a friend of a partner. We also offer couples packages where we decorate the room for a romantic ambiance, and serve you a bottle of wine or champagne along with some light snacks to enjoy in the privacy of your own room after the treatment.

What are your working hours

We are open every day of the week 10am to 10pm.

How to pick the perfect treatment

The best way to pick a treatment is by calling one of our very knowledgeable receptionists at 020 4547 4567. We will ask you the right questions to determine what treatment would be most suitable for your mood, physical needs, or occasion.

What facilities do you have at the spa

The SPA has 7 treatment rooms including a Hammam, 4 double rooms, a Thai room, and a single room. Every room is equipped with your own private shower, a relaxation area where you can have our complimentary drinks and snacks after every treatment, and essentials such as a make up removal kits, a hairdryer, male and female antiperspirant, single use hair brushes, and a large mirror.

You can view the spa and some of the treatment rooms via the Virtual Tour

Can i take a shower before or after the treatment

Of course! We have a shower in every single treatment room. If you would like to shower before or after the treatment, you are always welcome to do so. This comes at no extra charge, and does not reduce the length of the actual treatment up to 10 minutes of using the showers.

What if i feel uncomfortable about something during the treatment

If there is anything at all that you feel uncomfortable about, please ask the therapist to call the receptionist. We have a button in every room that when pressed, calls the receptionist to come immediately.

What is your refund policy

We refund 100% of the funds if the cancelation is done 24 hour prior to the booking, or reschedule a booking 16 hours prior to the booking. We do not refund gift/club cards. If you didn’t like you treatment, we will refund you the full amount if you interrupted the treatment half way through or earlier. If there is anything you would like to get in touch about, please reach out to our reception, or send an email to info@7skyspa.co.uk and we will get back to you within 1 working day.
